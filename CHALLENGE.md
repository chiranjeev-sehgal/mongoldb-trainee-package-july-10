# Debugging Olympics — Month 2: MongoDB Track

## Duration

45 minutes

## Objective

Fix the business defects in this repository without replacing the intended architecture. The codebase is expected to keep using Next.js API routes, MongoDB, Mongoose, and automated tests.

## Permitted Resources

- Official documentation
- Your own notes
- Approved AI tools if you disclose usage in your submission

## AI Policy

AI assistance is allowed only if you document what you used, the prompts you gave, and what output you rejected or corrected. Undeclared assistance may trigger an integrity review.

## Rules

- Do not modify fixture data.
- Do not modify visible tests to avoid failures.
- Do not disable linting or strict TypeScript checks.
- Do not replace database logic with static responses.
- Do not hardcode expected counts, titles, IDs, or result sets.
- Do not perform a complete rewrite of the repository.
- Keep the existing API contracts intact.

## Submission Requirements

- Commit your work to Git as you progress.
- Only work committed before the deadline counts.
- Record your final commit SHA in `SUBMISSION.md`.
- Complete every section of `SUBMISSION.md`.

- Functional correctness
